Frequently asked questions

Personal Plan is a subscription that gives you access to 28,000+ top-rated courses in tech, business, and more. You can learn at your own pace and cancel anytime.
With Personal Plan, you pay a monthly or annual subscription fee and get unlimited access to a curated collection of courses. Buying a course gives you lifetime access to that specific course for a one-time fee, with access to over 250,000 courses.
Courses in Personal Plan are selected based on quality, learner demand, and relevance to in-demand skills. Udemy's team works with top instructors to ensure you have access to the best content available.
You will be billed at the start of your subscription and on each renewal date thereafter — monthly or annually, depending on the plan you choose. You'll receive an email receipt after each payment.
You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. After cancellation, you'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.
Personal Plan includes a curated selection of 28,000+ courses. Not all of Udemy's 250,000+ courses are included — some courses are only available for individual purchase.
